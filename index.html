Our Heritage, Our Home Inc. is proud to announce the annual presentation of the Ohio Celtic Fest. The three-day family friendly festival is a completely unique festival experience and will showcase authentic Irish and International cultural activities.


Musicians from Ireland, Canada and points across the U.S. as well as local musicians will perform Irish dance and performances of traditional, folk and Irish rock music. Irish bagpipe bands, ceili dance bands, set dancing and ceili dance workshops will all be part of the fun. In addition to Irish music and dance presentations, the Ohio Celtic Festival will host thirty vendors of Irish products and twelve to fifteen food vendors. It is the goal of Our Heritage, Our Home, Inc. and The Ohio Celtic Fest to become one of the premiere Irish Festivals in Ohio attracting thousands of people to Lake County each year.


We are inviting you and your company to be a part of this annual festival by becoming a sponsor. The Festival is an outstanding opportunity to showcase your company's name and demonstrates the support you provide to the Lake County community.


The Ohio Celtic Fest is presented by Our Heritage, Our Home Inc., an Ohio 501C-3 corporation, so your sponsorship and/or donation to may be tax deductible to the extent allowed by law.
